ROBOTS

What Are Robots?

Robots are machine—especially one programmable by a computer—capable of carrying out a complex series of actions automatically.Robots can be guided by an external control device, or the control may be embedded within.

Types Of Robots By Chronology

In this case, up to five types of robots can be distinguished, in keeping with the stages that robotics has gone through until the present day.

  1. First generation: robot manipulators These can pick up and move objects but they have very restricted movements.
  2. Second generation: learning robots These gather information from the environment to make more complex movements.
  3. Third generation: reprogrammable robots These are equipped with sensors and they use programming languages to vary their functions in keeping with the needs of any given moment.
  4. Fourth generation: mobile robots The first intelligent robots capable of interpreting the environment in real time appear in the fourth generation.
  5. Fifth generation: robots with artificial intelligence This is the stage that’s currently under development. They’re intended to mimic human beings and they’re autonomous.

Importance of Robots

  • Improved safety conditions for labor:

    • Robots have changed the structure of society and made work environments safer.

  • Military and space exploration:

    • Advanced robotics in the military and NASA have transformed national defense and space exploration.

  • Efficiency and precision:

    • Robots can perform actions without mistakes and faster than humans.

  • Automation:

    • Once programmed, robots can perform different tasks, replacing human labor for labor-intensive jobs.
